\abstract{\textbf{Summary:} 
The pulseR R package is  designed to simplify processing of the data
 generated by RNA-seq pulse-chase experiments.
The package allows to work with various experimental schemes and to 
estimate kinetic parameters  such as degradation and synthesis rates
from read count data.\\
\textbf{Availability:} The pulseR package is freely
available at https://github.com/dieterich-lab/pulseR  under the
GPLv3.0 licence \\
